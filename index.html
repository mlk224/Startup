Who we are.


Saiki shirts, named after 'psyche' meaning 'the human soul, mind, or spirit', is an up-and-coming small business in Bethlehem, PA. Because we are a small business, we care about making the perfect shirt for our customers. Our team of designers will turn your inspiration photos into the shirt of your dreams!
